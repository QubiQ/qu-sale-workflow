=====================
Partner Sales Channel
=====================

Sales Channel and Division on partners. 


Installation
============

Just install.


Configuration
=============

Create divisions on Sales -> Configuration -> Partner Division. Create sales channels on Sales -> Configuration -> Sales Channels and assign divisions to them. Fill the 'Sales Channel' and 'Division' fields on the partner form. 


Usage
=====

The partner Sales Channel and Division will be transferred to Sale Orders and Sale Invoices.
